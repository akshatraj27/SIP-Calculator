SIP Calculator – Systematic Investment Plan Calculator

Prospective investors can think that SIPs and mutual funds are the same. However, SIPs are merely a method of investing in mutual funds, the other method being a lump sum. A SIP calculator is a tool that helps you determine the returns you can avail when parking your funds in such investment tools. Systematic Investment Plan or SIP is a process of investing a fixed sum of money in mutual funds at regular intervals. SIPs usually allow you to invest weekly, quarterly, or monthly.

What is SIP?

An SIP Calculator is an online tool that allows you to estimate the returns on your Mutual Fund investments, usually before you invest in them through one or more Systematic Investment Plans. It is simply a roadmap to help an investor seamlessly integrate SIP investments in his/her portfolio. However, the actual returns offered by a Mutual Fund scheme varies depending on various factors.
The manual computation can be slightly labor-intensive since SIPs typically involve a monthly payment. Since the holding period for each monthly contribution would be different at any given point in time, the returns for each of these payments will differ as well.

Benefits of SIP?

  • Disciplined Investing: Encourages a disciplined approach to investing by committing a fixed amount regularly.
  • Affordable: Investors can start with a small amount, making it accessible to everyone.
  • Lower Risk: Helps mitigate market volatility risk due to regular investments over time.
  • Goal-Oriented: SIPs can be aligned with specific financial goals such as retirement, education, or purchasing a home.
  • No Need to Time the Market: Regular investments mean you don't have to worry about market timing.
